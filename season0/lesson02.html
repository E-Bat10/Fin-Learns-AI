Teacher talk (2 min): Why the loop?

Great prompts aren’t magic— they’re a small routine. You tell AI what you want, your situation, and limits. It drafts. You critique it (like a coach), then it revises. Repeat until it’s useful.

  1. Goal — “Make a keeper warmup.”
  2. Context — “I’m 11, driveway or field, cones optional.”
  3. Constraints — “20 minutes, safe, 3 blocks.”
  4. Draft — first plan.
  5. Critique — “Missing high catches; add cues.”
  6. Revise — improved plan.
